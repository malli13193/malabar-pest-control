About pestico

Leading Pest Company Since 2008

MALABAR PEST CONTROL is a leading pest control company in the UAE with over 15 years of experience. We offer organic and non-organic pest control services in UAE, India, Oman, and North East Africa. Our services include General Public Health pest control, Termite Control, Fumigation, and Disinfection. We prioritize low-toxic, human- and pet-friendly, and eco-friendly pesticides. Stay assured of our high-quality service with the latest technology.
  • One of the leading organic pest control company in the region.
  • Vast years of experience in pest control field.
  • Timely updated technologies.
  • Guarantee service with regular follow ups.
  • 24/7 customer service.
  • Ecofriendly and pets friendly pesticides and are safe for kinds.
  • Covering both Residential and commercial facilities.
